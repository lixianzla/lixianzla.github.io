I  am a designer with a blend of design and research skills and a passion to tackle complex challenges. I have worked on user experience design, digital design and branding. Currently, I am a User Experience Designer in Edzy.

In 2017, I had an occasional opportunity to design a user interface of Singapore MRT weather monitoring system for the company called Nextant, where I found myself inspired by field of HCI.

From 2019 to 2020, I worked in SEDAC (a research consulting team at UIUC) as a research assistant, where I also took over lots of rebranding tasks because of my design skills. I had the chance to redesign the website and lots of digital materials.

Coming from interdisciplinary domains of study comprised of creative work and research methods, I have been thinking about resonating my design strategies deeply with analytic approaches to craft meaningful experiences.

I love to spend extensive time and collaboration efforts in coordinating and communicating within cross-functional teams. I eagerly strive to embody new ways of reducing the global carbon footprint and ensuring a safe, healthy, and sustainable built environment through research and design.
