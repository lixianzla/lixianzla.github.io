Overview

GO Street APP is a mobile application for street walking index measurement and walking navigation. The ultimate goal is to create a new street lifestyle and consumer ecosystem. Relying on the walking index, help users effectively find the places tailoring to their own preferences.


Market research

First, we looked at the general environment and trends related to national walking activities. Among them, mainly in the new infrastructure, the national health awareness of the promotion, urban humanization of the background, combined with relevant data show that walking and running is the main mode of exercise in the country accounted for a large population base (60%). And the national demand for walking travel and the demand for app-assisted travel or sports is increasing year by year.



Desk research

Next, we desk researched the topic of walking experience. Through the study of different urban spatial types and urban functions, we can explore what factors lead to good walking experience and bad walking experience.
