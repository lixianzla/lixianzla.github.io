Countdown

Timer that counts down in seconds, minutes, hours and days to any date.

Createx component
Date / time format to use inside data attribute:
data-countdown='MM/DD/YYYY HH:mm:ss AM/PM'

Example:

data-countdown='10/01/2022 07:00:00 PM'
